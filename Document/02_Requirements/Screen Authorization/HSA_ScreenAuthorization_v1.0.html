📌 Quy tac Nghiep vu Quan trong (Business Rules)

🔒Enrollment Gate: HV chi truy cap Course Home, Bai giang, Danh sach Bai thi khi Enrollment = CONFIRMED. Khi PENDING: chi xem trang thai dang ky, KHONG duoc nop bai.
💳Manual Payment (Phase 1): NV Ван hanh bat buoc XAC NHAN thanh toan thu cong truoc khi Confirm Enrollment. Khong co cong thanh toan online trong Phase 1.
🔓Giang vien Data Isolation: GV chi xem du lieu HV va ket qua thi cua cac Khoa hoc minh duoc PHAN CONG. Nghiem cam cross-access sang KH cua GV khac.
⏳Exam Anti-cheat: Trong khi lam bai, he thong phat hien tab-switching. Khong co chuc nang Pause. Auto force-submit khi Countdown = 0.
📧Parent Access: Phu huynh KHONG can tai khoan. Truy cap hoan toan qua Email link (time-limited token). Toan bo quyen la Read-Only (tru "Lien he Trung tam").
👔Giam doc: Read-only toan he thong. Quyen Write duy nhat: Phe duyet mo Khoa hoc moi (UC-GD-01). Khong duoc chinh sua truc tiep du lieu HV / GV.
🤖Adaptive Engine: Goi y AI chi cap nhat Competency Profile khi ti le cau tra loi trong bai thi > 30%. Bai bo cuoc khong duoc tinh.
✓QB Approval Gate: Cau hoi bat buoc gan du 3 metadata [Topic + Difficulty (Bloom) + Mon hoc] moi duoc Submit phe duyet. Chi NV Ван hanh moi co quyen Approve / Reject.
⚙Admin Role (v1.1 addition): Admin quan ly cac danh muc he thong (Mon hoc, Ma tran de, User accounts). Trong Phase 1, Quyen Admin co the do NV kiem nhiem. Day la role ky thuat, KHONG phai role nghiep vu End-user.
